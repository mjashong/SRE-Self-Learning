💾 Progress

🔄 Auto-save ON
Your progress saves automatically to this browser every time you finish a lesson, mission, or exercise. Just reopen the file and you're exactly where you left off — nothing to click.
📁 Auto-save to a folder
Pick a folder once (e.g. your Saved Progress folder). After that, a sre-lab-progress.json file is written there automatically on every change — a real backup you can sync via iCloud. Available in Chrome, Edge & Brave.
💼 Backup & restore
Save a portable progress file (drop it in your iCloud Saved Progress folder, or move it to another computer), or load one back.
⚠️ Reset everything
Clears all lessons, missions, exercises, and the study plan. Cannot be undone — export a backup first if unsure.
